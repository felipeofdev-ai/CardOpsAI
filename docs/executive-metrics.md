# Executive Metrics

## Board KPIs

- Processed volume and approval trends
- Revenue-at-risk and policy-level profit delta
- Chargeback stress exposure
- Capital reserve requirement and 12m capital forecast
- Automation rate and MTTR savings

## Risk Network KPIs

- Merchant influence score (network centrality)
- Fraud ring cluster counts and size distribution
- Temporal fraud concentration (hour/day/seasonality)

## Reliability and Controls

- Queue backpressure and DLQ rate
- Tenant quota breach rate
- Replay runtime and deterministic replay compliance
